About Fountain of Grace International

We believe no life is too broken for restoration. His grace is sufficient — and no one is left behind.

Who We Are

Fountain of Grace International (NPO 316-193) is a Christ-centered church in Pretoria North, South Africa. We exist to restore dignity, strengthen families, and reveal the hope of Jesus Christ to every generation.

Vision

A South Africa where every person encounters the grace of Jesus, experiences restoration, and walks in purpose.

Mission

  • Sharing the Hope of Jesus: Introducing people to the life-changing message of Christ.
  • Disciple-making: Equipping believers to grow in faith and multiply hope.
  • Compassionate Action: Meeting practical needs — poverty relief, recovery, empowerment — so no one is left behind.
  • Spiritual Growth: Through prayer, worship, and sound teaching, we help people live out God’s purpose.
