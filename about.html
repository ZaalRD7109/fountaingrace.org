About Fountain of Grace International

We believe no life is too broken for restoration. His grace is sufficient. No one is left behind.

Who We Are

Fountain of Grace International (NPO 316-193) is a Christ-centered church in Pretoria North, South Africa. We restore dignity, strengthen families, and reveal the hope of Jesus Christ to every generation.

Vision

A South Africa where every person encounters the grace of Jesus, experiences restoration, and walks in purpose.

Mission

  • Sharing the hope of Jesus: Introducing people to the life-changing message of Christ.
  • Disciple-making: Equipping believers to grow in faith and multiply hope.
  • Compassionate action: As a church, we serve our community through outreach, recovery support, and empowerment so no one is left behind.
  • Spiritual growth: Through prayer, worship, and sound teaching, we help people live out God’s purpose.
