About Fountain of Grace International

We exist to restore dignity, strengthen families, and point people to the hope of Jesus in Pretoria North, South Africa.

Vision

A South Africa where every person encounters the grace of Jesus, experiences restoration, and walks in purpose.

Mission

  • Sharing the hope of Jesus: We introduce people to the life‑changing message of Christ, fostering personal relationship with Him.
  • Disciple‑making: We equip believers for growth to become devoted followers who live out their faith.
  • Compassionate action: We build a welcoming community and serve through outreach—poverty relief, recovery support, and empowerment.
  • Spiritual growth: Through prayer, worship, and sound biblical teaching, we help people walk in God’s purpose.
