About Fountain of Grace International

We are a Christian Church in Pretoria North that believes no life is too broken for restoration. At Fountain of Grace International (NPO 316-193), His grace is sufficient — and no one is left behind. We exist to restore dignity, strengthen families, and reveal the hope of Jesus Christ to every generation.

Vision

A South Africa where every person encounters the grace of Jesus, experiences restoration, and walks in purpose.

Mission

  • Sharing the hope of Jesus: Introducing people to the life-changing message of Christ and nurturing personal relationship with Him.
  • Disciple-making: Equipping believers to grow strong in faith and multiply hope within their families and communities.
  • Compassionate action: Creating a welcoming community that meets practical needs — poverty relief, recovery support, and empowerment — so no one is left behind.
  • Spiritual growth: Through prayer, worship, and sound biblical teaching, we help people live out God’s purpose with courage and excellence.
