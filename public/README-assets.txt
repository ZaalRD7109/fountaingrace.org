REQUIRED FILES — add these to this /public folder before going live:

1. favicon.ico          — Your church favicon (32x32 or 16x16 .ico file)
2. icon.svg             — SVG version of your icon (any modern browser)
3. apple-touch-icon.png — 180x180 PNG for iOS home screen bookmark
4. og-image.jpg         — 1200x630 JPG for social media previews (Facebook, WhatsApp, Twitter/X)
                          Recommended: photo of your church building or service, with "Fountain of Grace International" text overlay

Without og-image.jpg, social media link previews will show a blank image.
Without favicon files, the browser tab will show a blank icon.

Use Canva (free) to create all of these quickly from your brand colors:
- Primary: #2a9df4 (sky blue)
- Accent: #ffd700 (gold)
