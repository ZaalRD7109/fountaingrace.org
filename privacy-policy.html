Privacy Policy

Fountain of Grace International (NPO 316-193) respects and protects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) and GDPR.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect your name, contact details, and any information you choose to share when contacting us, subscribing, donating, or volunteering.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and prayer requests
  • To process donations and send acknowledgements
  • To share ministry updates if you subscribe

3. Protection of Your Data

Your information is stored securely and used only for ministry and operational purposes. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party.

4. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing info@fountaingrace.org.

5. Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact us at info@fountaingrace.org.
