How to Give Safely to a Nonprofit in South Africa

A short guide so your generosity makes the most impact.

1) Check registration

Confirm the NPO number appears on the charity’s website and receipts. FGI’s is NPO 316‑193.

2) Look for transparency

Clear programs, real stories, and contact details build trust.

3) Give via secure platforms

Use recognised gateways like PayFast (ZAR) and PayPal (international).
