How to Give Safely to a Nonprofit in South Africa

A quick guide so your generosity makes the most impact.

1) Verify registration

Always confirm that the nonprofit displays its official NPO number on the website and receipts. Fountain of Grace International’s registration number is NPO 316-193.

2) Look for transparency

Trustworthy organisations share stories of impact, financial summaries, and real contact details. Transparency reflects integrity — a hallmark of genuine ministry.

3) Give through secure platforms

Use recognised and encrypted payment systems such as PayFast (ZAR) and PayPal (international). Avoid direct transfers to private accounts when donating online.

4) Request feedback

A healthy nonprofit welcomes accountability. After giving, feel free to ask for updates or join the community to see your impact.

5) Pray over every gift

Giving is spiritual before it is financial. Ask God to multiply your seed for Kingdom impact — and to bless the hands that serve with it.
