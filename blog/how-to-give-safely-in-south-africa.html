How to Give Safely to a Nonprofit in South Africa

A short guide so your generosity makes the most impact.

1) Check registration

Confirm that the NPO number appears on the charity’s website and receipts. Fountain of Grace International’s number is NPO 316-193.

2) Look for transparency

Clear programs, real stories, and visible contact details build trust. Genuine nonprofits share both impact reports and financial accountability.

3) Give via secure platforms

Use recognised gateways such as PayFast (ZAR) and PayPal (international) to ensure your donations are encrypted and trackable.
