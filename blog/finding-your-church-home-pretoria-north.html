Finding Your Church Home in Pretoria North

Searching for a simple, loving place to worship in Pretoria North? Fountain of Grace is a small church that meets in a home — where family, faith, and grace come first.

Where Your Search Truly Begins

Finding a church family isn’t about joining a crowd — it’s about finding a place where your heart feels at peace. At Fountain of Grace, we don’t have big buildings or fancy stages. We are three families who gather in a home, share meals, pray together, and let God do the growing.

If you’ve been looking for something real — not perfect — we might just be the church for you. Here, we believe every story matters and that no one is too broken for restoration.

Our Worship Style

Our worship is simple — we sing, we pray, and we share the Word of God. Sometimes it’s quiet, sometimes it’s joyful, but always led by the Holy Spirit. You’ll find no performance here, only presence.

Who We Are

We are a small, non-denominational Christian church in Pretoria North that believes in restoration through grace. We don’t have a rented hall or sound system — we gather as family in homes. What we do have is love, prayer, and faith in God’s provision for what’s next.

Visiting for the First Time?

Come as you are. Bring your children, bring your questions, bring your heart. There’s no dress code, no pressure — just people who love Jesus and each other.

What Makes Fountain of Grace Different

  • We’re real people — not performers.
  • We believe in prayer, not perfection.
  • We don’t have a church building — but we have a strong foundation: Christ.
  • We’re small enough to know you by name, and big enough to love you like family.
