Programs & Outreach

Each program at Fountain of Grace International is designed to restore dignity, build hope, and reveal the grace of Jesus in everyday life.

1️⃣ Food & Family Relief

Our relief ministry provides emergency food parcels, clothing, and basic care for struggling families across Pretoria North. Beyond provision, we bring prayer, encouragement, and community connection.

  • Weekly food parcel distribution
  • Partnership with local grocers and volunteers
  • On-site prayer and emotional support

2️⃣ Recovery & Prayer Support

Through the love of Jesus and confidential prayer support, we walk with those facing addiction, trauma, and emotional distress. His grace is sufficient — no one is left behind.

  • Prayer and counseling line
  • Small group recovery sessions
  • Faith-based mentorship and discipleship

3️⃣ Impact Seed Entrepreneurship

We train and empower aspiring entrepreneurs to start small, purpose-driven businesses. These initiatives create income, restore dignity, and fund community transformation.

  • AI-powered website creation training
  • Micro-enterprise coaching
  • Partnership opportunities for local investors
