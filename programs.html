Programs & Projects

Real help for real people in Pretoria North and beyond.

Food & Family Relief

Emergency food parcels, clothing, and referrals for families in crisis. We partner with local shops and donors to stretch every rand.

Recovery & Prayer

Confidential prayer and pastoral support. We walk with people seeking freedom from addiction, anxiety, or trauma and connect them to community.

Youth Mentorship

After‑school support, life skills, and discipleship to help young people build a hopeful future.

Entrepreneurship & Job Readiness

Micro‑enterprise training (Impact Seed), CV support, interview coaching, and connections to opportunities.
