# MODULE 1 — FULL STRATEGIC AUDIT
# Use: Quarterly. Maximum once every 3 months.
# Trigger: "Run Module 1."

---

## WHAT THIS MODULE DOES

Runs a complete health check of www.fountaingrace.org.
Produces a 90-day roadmap ranked by CEO Priority Stack.
Do not run this for small tasks — use Module 2 for that.

---

## AUDIT SCOPE

### VISITORS AND RANKINGS
- Google Business Profile: claimed, accurate, active posts, reviews responded to
- Target searches: where does FGI rank for each?
- robots.txt: allows correct pages, blocks nothing important
- sitemap.xml: present, accurate, submitted to Google Search Console
- Broken links: internal and external
- URL structure: clean, descriptive, no parameters
- Meta titles: unique, under 60 chars, keyword-rich
- Meta descriptions: unique, under 160 chars, compelling
- Heading structure: one H1 per page, logical H2/H3 hierarchy
- /llms.txt: present, current, factual

### TRUST
- About page: Ricardo Zaal named, photo present, role clear
- NPO number (316-193 NPO) visible on About and Donate pages
- Privacy Policy: exists, POPIA compliant, linked in footer
- Google reviews: count, average, response rate
- On-site testimonials: real, named, specific
- Impact stories: present and credible

### CONVERSIONS
- Homepage: clear next action visible above fold
- Plan Your Visit: page exists and is findable
- Prayer request: form works, submission confirmed
- Donate: page loads, payment options clear, credibility signals present
- WhatsApp CTA: present on key pages, correct wa.me format
- Email capture: form exists somewhere on site
- Every page has a next action for the visitor

### DONATIONS
- Donate page: headline, trust signals, payment methods, NPO proof
- Recurring giving option: present or absent
- Section 18A status disclosed honestly
- No friction on the path to donating

### RETENTION
- Email signup exists
- WhatsApp follow-up path exists
- Returning visitor has reason to come back (sermons, events, updates)

### RANKINGS AND CONTENT
- Key pages exist for all target searches
- Content decay check: old events, outdated dates, stale copy
- Internal linking: pages link to each other logically
- Open Graph tags: present on all pages, image correct size (1200x630px)

### SPEED AND PERFORMANCE
- Mobile PageSpeed score (target 85+)
- LCP (target under 2.5s)
- CLS (target under 0.1)
- INP (target under 200ms)
- Images: WebP format, compressed, explicit width/height
- LCP hero image: fetchpriority="high", not lazy loaded
- font-display: swap on all web fonts
- Critical CSS inlined in <head>
- GA4 and Clarity load after consent only

### CLOUDFLARE
- _headers file: CSP, HSTS, X-Frame-Options, X-Content-Type-Options, Referrer-Policy
- _redirects file: no chains, consistent www handling
- Static asset caching with long max-age
- Minification and Brotli compression enabled

### POPIA COMPLIANCE
- Cookie consent banner present
- GA4 and Clarity do not load before consent
- Privacy Policy covers: what is collected, how used, deletion rights
- Forms include data disclosure statement

### STRUCTURED DATA
- Organization, NGO, Church on homepage
- WebSite with SearchAction on homepage
- Person (Ricardo Zaal) on About page
- ContactPoint on Contact page
- Event on Events page
- Article on Sermons page
- FAQPage on FAQ page
- DonateAction on Donate page
- BreadcrumbList on all inner pages
- Validated at schema.org/validator

### AI SEARCH
- /llms.txt present and current
- Key facts stated within first 100 words of each page
- Entity "Fountain of Grace International" consistent across all pages
- Page purpose clear within first paragraph

---

## REQUIRED OUTPUT — IN THIS ORDER

1. **Scorecard** — grade each area A through F with one-line explanation
   Areas: Visitors | Trust | Conversions | Donations | Retention | Rankings | Speed | Security | AI Search

2. **Top 10 blockers** — what is actively hurting results right now, ranked by CEO Priority Stack

3. **Top 10 opportunities** — what could improve results most, ranked by ROI

4. **90-day roadmap** — week by week, CEO Priority order
   - Week 1–2: Highest impact fixes
   - Week 3–4: Trust and conversion improvements
   - Month 2: Rankings and content
   - Month 3: Speed, schema, and AI search

5. **12-month authority roadmap** — monthly milestones (not detailed tasks)

Apply Decision Matrix to every finding.
Rank everything by CEO Priority Stack.
Do not produce tasks outside this structure.
