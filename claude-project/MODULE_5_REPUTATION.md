# Use: When trust signals are weak, Google reviews need attention, or you want more social proof.

## WHAT THIS MODULE DOES

Strengthens the signals that make people trust FGI — online and on the site.
Trust is a ranking signal, a donation signal, and a conversion signal.
Do not skip this module. It directly affects visitors, donations, and rankings.

## TRUST SIGNALS INVENTORY

Audit these and flag what is missing or weak:

**Google Business Profile**
- Profile claimed and verified
- Address shows Pretoria North (not Akasia)
- Phone number correct and consistent with site
- Website URL correct
- Category: Church (primary), Nonprofit Organization (secondary)
- Photos: at least 10 (exterior, interior, team, community events)
- Posts: at least one per month
- Q&A: seeded with 5+ useful questions and answers
- Reviews: responded to within 48 hours
- Average rating and review count

**On-Site Trust Signals**
- About page: Ricardo Zaal named, photo present, role stated
- NPO number (316-193 NPO) visible on About and Donate pages
- Registration date mentioned where relevant
- Privacy Policy linked in footer
- Contact information consistent across all pages (NAP rule)
- Real photos of the team and community (not stock images)
- Service times and location clearly stated

**Social Proof**
- Testimonials: real names, specific outcomes, not vague praise
- Impact stories: community helped, food distributed, lives changed (with permission)
- Transparency update: simple summary of what FGI did this year

**Donor Trust**
- Section 18A status disclosed honestly (not yet approved — say so)
- How donations are used: stated clearly on donate page
- Recurring giving option explained
- Who to contact with questions about donations

## REQUIRED OUTPUT — BASED ON FOCUS STATED

The output depends on what focus is given. Examples:

**If focus is Google reviews:**
- Current state assessment
- Template for asking a church member for a Google review (plain, not pushy)
- Template for responding to positive reviews
- Template for responding to negative reviews
- Suggested Q&A seeds for the GBP profile

**If focus is testimonials:**
- What makes a good church testimonial (specific, real, impactful)
- Script for asking a member to share their story
- How to format it on the website
- Where to place it for maximum conversion impact

**If focus is the About page:**
- Full rewritten About page copy
- Structure: who FGI is, who Ricardo Zaal is, what drives the mission, why Pretoria North
- Real and specific — no generic church language
- Ends with an invitation to visit or connect

**If focus is GBP posts:**
- Two complete posts ready to copy and publish
- Each post: 150–300 words, clear topic, ends with one action
- Written for local Pretoria North audience

## RULES FOR THIS MODULE

- Do not fabricate any testimonials, reviews, statistics, or impact numbers
- Do not claim Section 18A status that has not been approved
- All copy must reflect the verified brand facts from Project Instructions
- Reputation is long-term — never sacrifice it for a short-term ranking tactic
