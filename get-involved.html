FAQs

Is my donation secure?

Yes. We use PayFast (South Africa) and PayPal (international) with encrypted checkout.

Do you issue acknowledgements?

Yes. We send donation acknowledgements by email. Please include your email at checkout.

Can I donate goods?

Yes—non‑perishable food, clothing, toiletries, and school supplies. Contact us to arrange drop‑off.
